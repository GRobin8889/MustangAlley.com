Current Models

2021 Mustang

Hear the roar of a Mustang as the ground starts to tremble and your legs start to shake. As always, Mustang calls upon its performance roots with features for enhanced handling, high-powered engine options and classic Mustang design.


2021 Mustang Mach 1

New for 2021, the soul-stirring Mustang Mach 1 and Mach 1 Premium stand at the pinnacle of 5.0L performance. The completely customizable Mach 1 continues its legacy, engineered specifically for quick turns and spirited drives. Mach 1 performance is clear even before you get behind the wheel. Its streamlined design and exterior styling cues celebrate Mach 1 heritage dating back more than five decades.


2021 Mustang Mach-E

Built from all the passion of its iconic heritage, Mustang Mach-E is the new shape of freedom. Get ready for 0–60 thrills, expansive range, zero vehicle emissions and more from the 2021 North American Utility Vehicle of the Year. The joy of driving is revitalized with the Mustang Mach-E Select. This model is home to one truly iconic design paired with luxurious materials and standard driver-assist technologies like Ford Co-Pilot360™ 2.0. Plus, all the open-road excitement you can handle, with access to the largest public charging network in North America offered by automotive manufacturers.


2021 Mustang Shelby GT500

The combination of a supercharged 5.2-liter V8 engine and a dual-clutch TREMEC 7-speed transmission sets up this Shelby for awe-inspiring power and performance. Designed with fighter jets in mind, the aerodynamic exterior helps get you to a top spedd of 186 mph. With its precise build and superior powertrain, the Shelby GT500 takes performance to a new heights as the fastest street legal mustang ever.
